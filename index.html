I am a Ph.D student in the Management Science unit, under the supervision of Prof. Timo Kuosmanen, at Aalto University School of Business. My research interests lie at the intersection of nonparametric econometrics, optimization, and production economics and their applications to resources and environmental fields. I will graduate in June 2022. Befor joining Aalto University BIZ, I earned my MSc. degree in Management Science from China University of Geosciences.
